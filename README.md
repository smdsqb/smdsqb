## What I've Built

### [Warditor](https://warditor.vercel.app) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [↗ repo](https://github.com/smdsqb/Warditor)
An AI-powered Android productivity app that doesn't block distractions — it makes you read exactly what you did the next morning. Built with a co-founder as a proper startup: OAuth integrations (Google, Discord, Spotify, GitHub), Razorpay payments, Firebase backend, and Gemini 2.5 Flash generating personalised daily shame reports.

`Flutter` `Next.js` `Firebase` `Supabase` `Gemini 2.5 Flash` `Razorpay`

---

### [IGCSEPulse](https://igcse-pulse.vercel.app) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [↗ repo](https://github.com/smdsqb/IGCSEPulse)
A full RAG pipeline built for IGCSE students. Upload past papers, ask questions, get curriculum-accurate answers. Built solo in 15 days during exam season because nothing useful existed. Mistral for OCR, Cohere for embeddings, Pinecone as the vector store, Groq for inference.

`Next.js` `Mistral` `Cohere` `Pinecone` `Groq` `Firebase`

---

### [Vexus](https://github.com/smdsqb/Vexus) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [↗ repo](https://github.com/smdsqb/Vexus)
A custom jersey e-commerce store built with a classmate — I handled all the tech, he handled the business side. Currently paused during exam season, resuming post-IGCSEs.

`Next.js` `Tailwind`
